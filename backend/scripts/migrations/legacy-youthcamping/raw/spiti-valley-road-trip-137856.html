    Overview

    Experience an amazing journey through Himachal Pradesh! explore quaint villages like Sangla, Chitkul, and Tabo. Discover ancient monasteries and stunning landscapes. Visit Kaza, home to unique places like Hikkim, Komic, Langza, and Kibber, and don't miss the breathtaking Chandrataal Lake. Finally, explore Manali before heading back to your city, with unforgettable memories of your adventure in the Himalayas!
    Note: Double sharing is not applicable for Chandratal/Losar stays.

    10 Nights 11 Days 23 Jun, 30 Jun, 07 Jul, and more Sabarmati, Gujarat
    Itinerary
    Day 1

    Train Journey Ahmedabad to Chandigrah

    Gandhinagar
    +
    • Boarding : Sabarmati BG Railway Station /  Gandhinagar Capital Railway Station
    • Reporting Time: 09:00 AM
    • Kickstart your adventure with an overnight train journey from Ahmedabad to Chandigarh, passing through Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Haryana. Enjoy scenic views, bond with fellow travelers through fun activities, and soak in the local flavors at stops like Abu Road and Jaipur

    Please note that if you have a connecting train from any station in Ahmedabad or Gandhinagar to Surat, Baroda, Mumbai, or Pune inter-station transfers are not included in our package. 

    Day 2

    Drive to Shimla & day tour of Shimla

    Shimla
    +
    • Arrival in Chandigarh – 8:00 AM
    • Reach Chandigarh Railway Station in the early morning.
    • Depart for Shimla by Traveler/Taxi, enjoying the scenic drive.
    • Explore Shimla’s famous Mall Road and shop for souvenirs.
    • Experience the lively nightlife at Shimla’s cafés and markets. 

    Stay: Hotel in Shimla

    Day 3

    Shimla to Chitkul, last village on Indo-Tibetan border

    Chitkul
    +
    • Shimla to Chitkul – The Ultimate Road Trip
    • Kickstart an adventurous morning road trip from Shimla.
    • Drive through the stunning landscapes of Kinnaur Valley.
    • Visit Chitkul, the last village on the old Hindustan-Tibet trade route. 

    Stay: Cottage in Chitkul/Kalpa

    Food: Breakfast,Dinner Included

    Day 4

    Travel from Chitkul to Tabo, via Nako Lake

    Tabo
    +
    • Chitkul to Tabo – A Scenic Himalayan Drive
    • Begin the journey from Chitkul, experiencing the serene village life.
    • Drive along the India-Tibet border, passing through Khab, the confluence of the Spiti & Sutlej Rivers.
    • Stop by the beautiful Nako Lake for a peaceful retreat. 

    Stay: Homestay in Tabo

    Food: Breakfast,Dinner Included

    Day 5

    Explore Tabo and Dhankar Village

    Kaza
    +
    • Tabo to Kaza – Exploring Ancient Monasteries & Scenic Villages
    • Visit the 1000-year-old Tabo Monastery, known for its ancient wall paintings and mud statues.
    • Witness a breathtaking 360° sky view from Tabo.
    • Explore Dhankar Village & Gompa, perched on a cliff with stunning valley views.
    • Travel to Kaza, the headquarters of Spiti Valley.
    • Stroll through Kaza Market, soaking in the local vibes.

    Stay: Homestay in Kaza

    Food: Breakfast,Dinner Included

    Day 6

    Explore Key, Komic, Langza, and Hikkim in a day

    Kaza
    +
    • Exploring the Wonders of Spiti Valley
    • Visit Key Monastery, the largest Tibetan Buddhist monastery in Spiti, known for its murals, manuscripts, and spiritual aura.
    • Explore Komic, the world’s highest motorable village (15,050 ft), and its ancient Komic Gompa.
    • Witness the majestic Buddha Statue at Langza (14,300 ft) and discover fossils from an ancient seabed.
    • Send a postcard from Hikkim, home to the world’s highest post office (14,400 ft).

    Stay: Homestay in Kaza

    Food: Breakfast,Dinner Included

    Day 7

    Visit Kibber and Chicham, then head towards Chandra Taal

    Chandra Taal
    +
    • Spiti Valley to Chandra Taal – A Journey to the Moon Lake
    • Visit Kibber, a stunning high-altitude village known for its barren beauty and monasteries.
    • Cross the Chicham Bridge, the highest bridge in Asia, offering breathtaking views.
    • Travel to Chandra Taal, the mesmerizing "Moon Lake" on the Samudra Tapu plateau.
    • Overnight stay at Chandra Taal Camp.
    • Note: Chandra Taal Lake usually opens by late May or June. If inaccessible, we stay in Kaza and reroute via Kalpa and Shimla.

    Stay: Chandratal/Losar stay in tent or Homestay

    Food: Breakfast,Dinner Included

    Day 8

    Journey to Manali through the Atal Tunnel

    Manali
    +
    • Chandra Taal to Manali – A Thrilling Journey
    • Start the morning with a visit to the stunning Chandra Taal Lake.
    • Travel to Manali via Chhatru, experiencing one of the world’s most thrilling and scenic routes.
    • Pass through the famous Atal Tunnel and explore the breathtaking Solang Valley. 

    Stay: Homestay/Tent in Manali

    Food: Breakfast,Dinner Included

    Day 9

    Explore Manali & adventure activities

    Manali
    +
    • Manali Adventure & Sightseeing
    • Kickstart the day with thrilling adventure activities like paragliding and river rafting.
    • Explore Manali’s iconic spots, including Hadimba Devi Temple, Mall Road Market, and the charming Old Manali area.
    • Night drive to jalandhar

    Food: Breakfast Included

    Day 10

    Reach Jalandhar & then board the return train

    Jalandhar
    +
    • Enjoy the overnight journey back to Ahmedabad with new friends, memories, and thrilling experiences.
    • Share your photos, videos, and stories, and engage in fun activities on the way home.
    Day 11

    Arrival in Ahmedabad

    Home Town
    +
    • Arrival in Ahmedabad – Journey Concludes
    • Enjoy the famous Rabdi & Chaat as the train passes through Rajasthan in the morning.
    • Time to bid farewell, cherish the memories, and conclude this incredible journey!
