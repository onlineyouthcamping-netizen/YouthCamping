    Overview

    Spiti in winter is a world straight out of a postcard—blanketed in pristine white snow, frozen rivers, and towering Himalayan peaks. The valley transforms into a serene, untouched paradise where ancient monasteries stand tall amidst snow-capped mountains, and quaint villages like Kaza, Langza, and Hikkim feel like time has slowed down. With crisp mountain air, star-studded nights, and the thrill of winter roads, Spiti offers a rare adventure for those who crave raw beauty and tranquility.

    Highlights
    What makes this trip special

    •  Experience Spiti in Winter – Snow-covered valleys, frozen rivers, and stunning Himalayan landscapes.

    •  Visit Asia’s Highest Bridge – Chicham Bridge, with breathtaking views of deep gorges.

    •  World’s Highest Post Office – Send a postcard from Hikkim, a village above 14,000 ft.

    • Ancient Monasteries – Explore Key and Dhankar Monasteries, rich in Tibetan culture.

    • Authentic Homestay Experience – Stay with locals and enjoy traditional Himachali hospitality.

    • Thrilling Winter Road Trip – Scenic drives through snow-lined roads and mountain passes.

    • Starry Night Skies – Witness some of the clearest night skies and countless stars.

    • Local Food & Culture – Taste Spitian delicacies and experience the warmth of mountain life.

    • Bonfire Nights – Relax around a warm bonfire, share stories, and bond with travelers.

    • Instagram-Worthy Landscapes – Perfect backdrops for photography lovers and adventure seekers.

    FAQ

    What Will Be the Group Size for This Spiti Tour?  

    ​We like to keep our groups small, with just 15-20 people. This way, everyone can get to know each other better and work together more easily. Smaller groups make it easier to coordinate the trip and have a more collaborative experience. It's simpler to bond with everyone when there aren't too many people around.

    Who All Will Be Accompanying The Group During The Himachal  Tour?

    We always have skilled Trip Leads, Assistant Trip Leads, and a Driver with us on our trips. They are fully trained and ready to help out whenever needed. Sometimes, we also have Local Guides and Porters with us if we need extra help during our journey or trek.

    Being The Only Girl In The Group, Will The Journey Be Safe For Me As Well?

    Absolutely! Don't worry about traveling alone as a solo girl. You should feel confident joining us on the departure date. We'll take care of you throughout the entire journey. Our trip leads are trained to handle different situations, and they'll be there to support you every step of the way.

    What Type of Ground Transportation Will Be Used on the Trip?

    We want you to feel comfortable throughout the journey, which is why we make sure to use top-notch vehicles that suit the terrain and your needs. You might travel in vehicles like Tempo Traveller or taxis such as Innova, Bolero, Tata Sumo, and more.

    Do I Need to Buy Trekking Shoes for the spiti Tour?

    No, sports shoes with good grip will work.

    What Are the Washroom/Toilet Facilities on the Himachal Tour?

    During our trip, we'll stay in different types of accommodations like hotels, camps, and homestays. We've made sure that all these places have washrooms with running water and proper sanitation facilities.

    What Type of Food Is Served During the Trip?

    We make sure to give you good, clean, and healthy vegetarian food during the trip.
