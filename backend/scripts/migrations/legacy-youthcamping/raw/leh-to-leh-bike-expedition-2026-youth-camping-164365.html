    Overview

    Experience the raw beauty of the Himalayas on this thrilling 7 Days / 6 Nights adventure through the heart of Ladakh. Starting and ending in Leh, this journey takes you across some of the world’s highest motorable roads, stunning valleys, peaceful monasteries, and crystal-clear alpine lakes.

    Ride through the legendary Khardung La Pass, explore the cold desert landscapes of Nubra Valley, visit the charming border village of Turtuk, and witness the magical blue waters of Pangong Lake.

    Designed for solo travelers, couples, and groups of friends, this community-style trip offers comfortable stays in hotels and camps, delicious meals, professional trip leaders, mechanical backup, and complete safety support — making it perfect for both first-time riders and adventure lovers.

    Whether you’re chasing mountain views, cultural experiences, or unforgettable road journeys, this Leh to Leh expedition promises memories that will stay with you forever.

    Nubra Valley

    • Explore Hunder Sand Dunes

    • Check-in to camps in Nubra Valley
    • Dinner & overnight stay

    • Highlights of the day:Khardung La Pass Giant Maitreya Buddha at Diskit Monastery Double-humped camel ride at Hunder Sand Dunes Nubra Valley landscapes

    NUBRA VALLEY TO PANGONG (180 KM)
