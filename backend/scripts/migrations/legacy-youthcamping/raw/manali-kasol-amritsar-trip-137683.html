    Overview

    Get ready for an unforgettable journey through Northern India! Begin with a train journey from your city to Jalandhar. Explore the cultural richness at Wagah Border and the serene beauty of the Golden Temple in Amritsar. venture to Kasol for a relaxing experience and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of the Kasol market. Don't forget to visit the tranquil Manikaran Gurudwara and trek to the picturesque Chalal village.

    Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Bhrigu Lake trek, which offers stunning views amidst lush green meadows and dense forests. Upon reaching Manali, explore its charm by visiting attractions like the Hadimba Devi Temple and the serene Vashisht Hot Springs. Take leisurely walks along the Mall Road, soaking in the scenic vistas.

    In Manali, explore the enchanting Solang Valley, known for its picturesque landscapes and serene ambiance. 

    To add an extra thrill to your journey, head to Kullu for exciting rafting adventures on the river.

    Conclude your trip with a scenic drive back to Jalandhar, before catching the train home, filled with cherished memories of your Northern India expedition. Get ready to embark on an enriching and unforgettable adventure!

    Only People of age 18 to 35 years of age are allowed to join this trip as it is a complete Trekking & backpacking itinerary and runs on a scheduled which is powerpacked.

    Train Journey to Firozpur/Kotkapura

    Visit Wagha Border & Golden Temple

    Amritsar

    Night Drive to Manali

    Day 3

    Day for Kasol & Parvati valley exploration

    Kasol
    +
    • Arriving at Kasol early morning and check-in, Rest and have hot Breakfast & Fresh-up.

    • Visit Manikaran Gurudwara located at the height of 5700FT & you can have a Sacred bath in hot springs at gurudwara.
    • Enjoy hot meal for lunch at camp & take rest for few hours.

    • Visit Kasol Market & Explore cafes of Kasol, short hike to chalal village via Parvati Valley & don't forget to click amazing photos at chalal bridge, visit to musical cafe as you reach chalal village.

    • back to camp an exquisite dinner, camping near parvati river under the million stars.

     

    Night stay: Riverside Camping In Kasol or Cottages

    Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner Included

    Day 4

    Start Bhrigu Lake trek(Summer)

    Bhrigu lake base Camp
    +
    •  After having breakfast early in the morning depart to Bhrigu Base Camp. Leave all your luggage at Manali & trek with a small backpack(water, snacks, raincoat) & warm clothing.

    • After reaching base vilage will start Bhrigu Lake Trek & stay in Bhrigu lake base Camp around 10000FT with mesmerizing views, The meadows on the Bhrigu Lake trek stretch far and wide – almost like an endless carpet.

    Night Stay: Tent stay in Bhrigu Campsite

    Meals: Breakfast, pack Lunch, Dinner included

    Day 5

    Bhrigu Lake summit

    Advance Base Camp
    +
    • You begin with hiking to the first ridge, which is an hour away.

    • In April, May, and June, the trail is usually covered in snow. In July, as the snow melts on the lower ridges you see the trail slowly turning from green to brown and white.

    • A special day because today we will trek to 14300 ft height and get a splendid view of Bhrigu Lake. This day ends with a summit party.

    Night stay: Tent stay in Bhrigu Campsite

    Meals: Breakfast, pack Lunch, Dinner

    • Dinner and night stay 

    Night stay: Cottage/Tent in Kullu

    Meals: Breakfas, Lunch, Dinner Included

    FAQ

    Who All Will Be Accompanying The Group During The Himachal  Tour?

    We always have skilled Trip Leads, Assistant Trip Leads, and a Driver with us on our trips. They are fully trained and ready to help out whenever needed. Sometimes, we also have Local Guides and Porters with us if we need extra help during our journey or trek.

    Being The Only Girl In The Group, Will The Journey Be Safe For Me As Well?

    Absolutely! Don't worry about traveling alone as a solo girl. You should feel confident joining us on the departure date. We'll take care of you throughout the entire journey. Our trip leads are trained to handle different situations, and they'll be there to support you every step of the way.

    What Type of Ground Transportation Will Be Used on the Trip?

    We want you to feel comfortable throughout the journey, which is why we make sure to use top-notch vehicles that suit the terrain and your needs. You might travel in vehicles like Tempo Traveller or taxis such as Innova, Bolero, Tata Sumo, and more.

    Do I Need to Buy Trekking Shoes for the Himachal Tour?

    Since we'll be trekking to Himachal , it's important to have the right shoes. You'll need trekking shoes, especially ones with good soles that are comfortable to wear. If you don't have trekking shoes, sports shoes with good grip will also work.

    What Are the Washroom/Toilet Facilities on the Himachal Tour?

    During our trip, we'll stay in different types of accommodations like hotels, camps, and homestays. We've made sure that all these places have washrooms with running water and proper sanitation facilities.

    What Type of Food Is Served During the Trip?

    We make sure to give you good, clean, and healthy vegetarian food during the trip.

    Do You Prefer I Should Bring Some Medicines With Me?

    It's a good idea to bring your own medication for the Himachal trip. But don't worry, we also have first aid boxes available for common symptoms, and all our trip leads know basic medical practices to help if needed.
